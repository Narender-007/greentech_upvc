Who We are

We Are GREENTECH

From the initial specification through to completion, Greentech is committed to keeping all stakeholders informed of the progress, issues and details required to meet everyone’s expectation. Managing expectations is a fundamental belief at Green Tech, as clear written communication is vital to the completion of any successful project. Combining disciplined communication practices with the latest technologies makes Greentech business model the most unique in the window industry. We have a vibrant group of craftsmen for production and installation supported by our colleagues with experience of more than 15 years in the uPVC Industry. For architect and contractor, Greentech offers simple and comprehensive process that avoids the multi-layered structure of the established uPVC window industry in South India.ices. Our extensive experience in the luxury residenal, historical replacement and bouque commercial markets allows us to offer a unique design-assist service at all stages of construction.
